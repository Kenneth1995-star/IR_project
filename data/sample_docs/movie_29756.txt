Cooliekkaran

VK lives with his elder brother (Nagesh), sister-in-law (Srividya), and their little daughter. He spends his time idle. He is scolded by his brother,and so VK becomes a coolie and transports articles. He is jailed for smuggling. His brother gets into an accident at work at the mill and loses his arms. He loses his job and needs money for treatment. VK is released. VK begs his brother's boss (Radharavi), but he refuses. VK breaks into the boss' safe to get the money but it is too late as the brother dies. The boss throws VK in jail. This is where he meets the men and decide to seek revenge. In jail, VK meets the warden (Jai), a smart, ethical, strong man, whose daughter was kidnapped by one of the prisoners when she was little. After his release, he is a different, stronger man. He and his accomplices steal the gold that the boss had been smuggling at the mill. VK, his accomplices lie to his sister-in-law. They move from poverty into riches. VK and his men snatch a diamond which is very rare and he wins it. Roopini steals it (fake diamond). Roopini’s father (Vijayan) is killed by Radharavi. Vijayan was once a henchmen of Radharavi. Vijayan was assigned to kill Jai and his family in an accident. Jai’s wife is killed and Vijayan keeps the child with him. Roopini is now reformed. Vk and Roopini fall in love and for their engagement he buys all the flowers in the city so Jai grew suspicious and starts following VK. Radharavi buys a counterfeit machine which is spoilt by VK enraged by this Radharavi spills the beans to Vk’s sister-in-law (Srividya) that VK is a smuggler. Srividya urges Vk to leave this business. The boss throws VK in jail.How he escapes and gets his revenge on the boss forms the climax of the movie
