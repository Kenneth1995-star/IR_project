Black Hunters

It shows four 21st century treasure seekers who travel in time to the Second World War in Russia. (the former Soviet Union in 1942.) They dig near St. Petersburg in search of hidden medals and honors of soldiers who fought during the war. The chief of the group is Bormann(Danila Kozlovsky), who is a former student of the Faculty of History Sergei Filatov. His assistant is a Rastafarian nicknamed Alcohol (Andrei Terentyev). Two other members are Chuhat (Dmitry Volkostrelov) and Skull (Vladimir Yaglych). Skull is a neo-Nazi and has a stylized tatto of the swastika on his shoulder.
