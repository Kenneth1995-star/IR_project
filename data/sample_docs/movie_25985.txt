Heeralal Pannalal

Heeralal Pannalal is a comedy entertainer with Mithun and Johnny starring in Parallel roles. It is the story of "two sets" of "twins" and both hve been named "Heeralal" and "Pannalal"; they set out in search of love and adventure, but they are quite unaware of the presence of the other "set" of Heeralal and Pannalal.
The film resembled the Amithabh-Govinda starrer Bade Miyan Chote Miyan, directed by David Dhawan.
