Bhairav

Rakesh Pandey was arrested for a Murder, which he never committed. While returning from office he was arrested for of killing a woman in cold-blood. He denies this charge, but in vein as the police produces witnesses and the court convicted him for seven years imprisonment. After releasing from jail Rakesh returns and finds that his parents killed. Rakesh started searching person behind the all. He is a wealthy industrialist and politically influenced person named Jindal. Rakesh vows to avenge and took the name of Bhairav.
