Shyam

The film which revolves around friendship and love narrates the story of Shyam (Rahul Madhav), who lost his father in a mishap when he was young and the developing unique relationship with his mother Rajamma (Sajitha Madathil) and mutual friends, who supports him during difficult situations.[3][4] Shyam works as an accountant. He is liked and loved by others. Sebin (Bhagath Manuel) is a supervisor in a car company, Stephen (Govindankutty) works as a medical representative and Ramachandran (Vivek Gopan) is a sales executive. The incidents that took place in the lives of these bachelors is form the plot. The film portrays how one's childhood experiences influence the future and personality of others and the value of relations in one's life.[5][4][6]
