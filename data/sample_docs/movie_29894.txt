Pavunnu Pavunuthan

K Bhagyaraj plays the role of a commando captain who visits his hometown after many years of his elope in childhood. He keeps his identity secret to all villagers. He witnesses his sister, father and lover anxiously anticipating for him for years. His love interest Pavunnu(Rohini) ignores him and blasts him as and when she encounters him. K.Bhagyaraj too playfully bullies her and teases her. But who is Bhagyaraj really? Will he marry Pavunnu forms rest of the story. Story was directed in Poolampatti village between Mettur to Edappadi Pavunnu Pavunuthan is a Tamil film written and directed by K. Bhagyaraj.[1][2]
