Anandabhairavi

Jayaraj's Anandabhairavi depicts the life of Vasudeva Panikker, a Kathakali artist, and his son. Sai Kumar plays the role of Panikker and Master Devan is his son Appu. Sai Kumar won the Kerala State award for the second best actor, for his performance in this movie. Storyline is prepared jointly by Mahesh and Sajeev. Madambu Kunjukkuttan scripted the movie. The music is by Parthasarathi. Harinath cranked the camera.
