Kilukkam Kilukilukkam

Chandini (Kavya Madhavan) sees her father Krishnadas (Vijayaraghavan), Mayor of Kochi being killed by the rivals, but manages to escape from them and comes to Ooty. The needle of suspicion falls on Royichan (Kunchacko Boban) and friend Appachan (Salim Kumar). So they shift to Ooty to escape. Meanwhile, Balu(Jayasurya) and Ponnappan (Harisree Ashokan) sees a lost child information and goes to ooty expecting her parents to give them lots of money. Nischal (Jagathy Sreekumar) captivated by the beauty of Chandini is also in Ooty trying to impress her. Meanwhile, Chandini goes missing. Rest of the story is how Joji (Mohanlal) gets involved and the way he leads the team to trace and save Chandini.
