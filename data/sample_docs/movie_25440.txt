Hiraasat

Hiraasat is an action film, featuring Mithun Chakraborty and Anita Raj in lead roles, well supported by Shatrughan Sinha, Hema Malini, Prem Chopra, Kalpana Iyer and Shakti Kapoor. Shatrughan Sinha played the character of an honest police office who could sacrifice everything for his country.
