Paribhavam[1] (പരിഭവം)

Paribhavam tells the story of Chandu (Abhilash) and Nandini (Krupa) who are orphans.
Nandini (Kripa) had to come back to Kerala after her parents were killed in US. She develops a fascination for a mute boy (Abhilash) and soon moves into the city with him. Their public displays of affection makes the locals agitate against them.
