Mooru Guttu Ondu Sullu Ondu Nija

This is a less expensive comedy movies with the overall movie being shot in a single house. The movie starts with the family having four children with komal as eldest son and three girls. The overall movie is drama taking place in house in one day. The parents are scheduled to a one-day trip to Tirupati. The children knowing about it the elder three children inform their lovers to come to their home.[citation needed]
But the comedy starts when their aunt comes to sleep in their home. The lovers are supposed to hide in their rooms so that they don't fall in eyes of other siblings and aunt. After sometime the appu (Ramesh Aravind) arrives with some relational links and sleeps in their home. He visits each of the children's room and the interesting is to watch how each of them hide their lovers in their home. Finally, when next day when parents arrive various truth are brought in front of them with some feelings.[citation needed]
