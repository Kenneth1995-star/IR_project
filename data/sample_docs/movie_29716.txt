Pillai Nila

Dolly (Raadhika) is the young sister of a widowed rich man David (Jaishankar). She falls in love with Mogan (Mogan) a married man. However, he rejects her, because his wife, Bhuvaneswari (Nalini) is pregnant. Dolly's obsession causes her to commit suicide, in front of him, at the hospital, at the same time as his wife is giving birth. Their daughter, Shalini (Baby Shalini) is imbued with Dolly's evil spirit as Dolly takes revenge. But Mohan is save his family by praying Kottess Amman.
