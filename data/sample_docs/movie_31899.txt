Bhale Donga

A story begins with a swashbuckling thief Surendra (Nandamuri Balakrishna), also a master of disguise, who always targets Vidhatha (Charan Raj) dictator of the city and he is being tracked down by SP Indrani (Sharada), an efficient Police officer. Surendra contracts a multi-speciality hospital for labor at free of cost with all these money. Dr.Rekha (Vijayashanti), the younger sister of Indrani, inspired to the Surendra's good heart and starts loving him.
After several other successful attempts, one day Surendra is caught to Rekha, when she questions him then he reveals his past his father (Ranganath) an honest person, who had collected donations from the people of the town for construction of hospital with his partner Vidhatha. Vidhatha has double-crossed and stolen entire account, keeping the blame on his father, which led to his suicide. That's why Surendra is taking revenge against Vidhatha.
In an attempt Surendra is trapped and arrested by Indrani, she tells him that he had been for his welfare only, because she has a personal fight with Vidhatha, whose actual name is Vijay was her ex-lover, who had killed her parents and escaped from imprisonment. Now Surendra & Indrani joins together. Whether they able take revenge against Vidhatha & Surendra able complete the hospital? That forms the crux of the rest of the story.
