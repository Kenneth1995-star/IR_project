Royal Treasure

When Huang Bo, Sun Honglei, Huang Lei, Show Lo, Wang Xun and Zhang Yixing are filming the reality show Go fighting!, a thunderstone had struck them, and they go back to the Ming dynasty (1368 - 1644). They are in favour with the emperor, before the death of the emperor, he leaves the legacy - the Order of the Holy Flame to them. After the emperor died, they return to the modern civilization and begin their journey of searching for the treasure.[2]
