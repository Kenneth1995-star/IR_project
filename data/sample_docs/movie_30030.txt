Rojavai Killathe

Alexander (Arjun) is a henchman who works for the smuggler Peter (Tiger Prabhakar) and Alexander is considered by Peter as his faithful henchman. Peter often confronts his enemy Ayyanar (Radha Ravi). Ayyanar forces the innocent Anu (Kushboo) to dance in stage and he ill-treats her. Peter orders Alexander to kill the Ayyanar's weak point Anu. Alexander cannot kill her and finally shoots on her shoulder. Wounded, Anu is admitted to hospital. Then, Alexander decides to save her from Peter and Ayyanar.
