Chase a Crooked Shadow

In her family's Spanish villa, Kimberly Prescott (Baxter), a young South African heiress of a diamond company, is grieving after her father's recent suicide, when she is taken aback by the arrival of a man (Todd) claiming to be her brother Ward, believed to have died in a car accident a few months ago. Kimberly has trouble convincing her friends and family and the local police inspector (Lom) that a complete stranger has taken her deceased brother's identity and appears to know events of their shared childhood. Finally, there is a dramatic twist in the tale.
