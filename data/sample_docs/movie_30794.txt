Piragu

Sathya (Hamsavardhan) is a drama director in his village who lives with his father Ramaiah, a respected Koothu artist, and his mother. The village belle Thulasi (Keerthi Chawla), Sathya's relative, is in love with him since her childhood. Sathya then comes to Chennai with hopes of becoming a cinema director one-day. In Chennai, he lost his luggages and his contact addresses, Sathya, who now is left penniless, has nowhere to go. So, Sofia (Sunitha Varma), the owner of a small mechanic shop, gives him to eat and accommodates him in her mechanic shop. In turn, Sathya helps her to repair motorcycles besides looking for a film producer. Afterwards, Sofia falls in love with Sathya.
At last, impressed by Sathya's story a film producer accepts to producer his film. The same day, Sathya saves David (Karate Raja) from being killed by local rowdies. Then, the gang leader Antony (Kadhal Dhandapani) commends Sathya for saving his brother from the rival gang rowdies and offers him to join his gang. Sathya declines Antony's proposal and speaks ill of his activity. Thereafter, Sathya's parents and Thulasi arrive in Chennai, they are accommodated by Sofia in her house. In the meantime, Antony and the rival gang leader Annapoorani (Emey) make peace, and want to kill Sathya at all costs. What transpires next forms the rest of the story.
