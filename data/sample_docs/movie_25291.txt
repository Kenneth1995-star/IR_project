Hanste Khelte

Hanste Khelte is a murder mystery starring Mithun Chakraborty and Rakesh Roshan in the lead roles, well supported by Zarina Wahab, Raza Murad and Vijayendra Ghatge. Mithun (Mithun Chakraborty), Rakesh (Rakesh Roshan), and Vijay (Vijayendra Ghatge) are more interested in college political issues than their studies, which entangles them in a murder mystery.
