Arabia

Place Dharmapuri has got a very poor king who loves peace and happiness for his people.. But nearby Kalabai want to take away Dharmapuri from the king and people. Kalabai gives false evidence and gets permission from Sarkar. And Kalabai gives one week time for the king and people to run away from Dharmapuri. Now comes Babu antony as a merchant from Arabia. Actually Babu father was leader who was killed by Kalabai. Babu has now come to revenge for his father death. Film ends with Babu kill Kalabai and establish peace.
