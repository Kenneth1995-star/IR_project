Lost and Found

Lost & Found is basically a love story of Manas (Siddharth Chandekar) and Naina (Spruha Joshi) dealing with loneliness in their personal lives. Since loneliness has become a prominent part of our urban lives, Lost & Found empathizes ignored human emotions with a basic motive of bringing happiness in the lives of lonely people.
