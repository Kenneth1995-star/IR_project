Pidakkozhi Koovunna Noottandu

The story revolves around a paying guest home run by Chinthamani Ammal (K. P. A. C. Lalitha) who is a determined man-hater due to a failed love affair. Only man-haters are allowed as paying guests. Ponnamma (Kalpana), one of the paying guests, hates men because of her parents being partial to her twin brother Ponnappan(Kalpana). Another guest, Nancy (Vinaya Prasad), is a lawyer and is engaged on a post divorce custody battle for the child with her ex-husband Tony Varghese. Vasundhara (Rudra) and Bhagyarekha (Urvashi) pretend to be man-haters to please Chinthamani Ammal.
The story is about how each of the women finds true life partner. It is a super comedy entertainer.
