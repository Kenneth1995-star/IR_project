Pure Love

In 2014, a radio DJ gets a letter from his first love that brings up almost-forgotten memories of the past.
23 years ago in 1991, five friends spend the summer together. One of them, shy and innocent Beom-sil falls in love with Soo-ok. Beom-sil has a noticeable crush to Soo-ok and waits by the girl's window during the summer. Soo-ok suffers with a leg that can't make her walk properly so she is always carried by Boem-sil. The love blossoms and how he always sees her as the girl he had want to marry. .[5]
