Sabari

The film tells the story of Dr. Sabarivasan (Vijayakanth) who goes on a revenge spree against rowdy elements who are disturbing peace and tranquility in the city. Sabari treated the most wanted rowdy in the police department and finally sends back him to the police custody after the treatment. The gangster starts chasing Sabari for the revenge. The story revolves around the clash between Sabari and the gangster's groups.
