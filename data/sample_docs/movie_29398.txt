Kannan En Kadhalan

Kannan (MGR) is the adopted son of a retired Captain. Sundar (R. Muthuraman) is the real son. Mallika (Jayalalitha) is the niece of this rich man, who is engaged, at birth to Sundar. Mallika is secretly in love with Kannan. Malathi (Vanisri) lost her father in an accident; she now lives with the widow as her adopted daughter. Malathi and Kannan fall in love as they share good bonding. Kannan and Sunder aren't in good terms. Sundar's behavior towards Mallika and Malathi is not appropriate. Watch the movie Kannan En Kadhalan to know the destiny of this love Quadrilateral.
