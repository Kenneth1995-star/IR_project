Masterpiece

The protagonist of the film, Masterpiece, is Yuva (Yash), a local troublemaker,[2] who aspires to rule over everybody.[3] His mother,[4] wishes her son to be well-educated and a respected member of society,[2][3] but Yuva needs to learn the 'good' and 'bad' by living through the consequences of his own choices.[3] In the wake of his mother's dreams to see her son as a social reformer,[4] a unique love story unfolds between Yuva and Nisha (Shanvi Srivastava).[2]
