Solo

Gautham (Nara Rohit) is an orphan who always wishes to get married to a girl from a joint family to experience the love of relationships and elders. He falls in love with Vaishnavi (Nisha Aggarwal) who belongs to a joint family. He tries to woo her and finally she falls in love with him. Vaishnavi shares a very good relationship with everyone at home, especially her dad Naidu. One day he finds out about his daughter and Gautham's relationship. He dislikes it as he wants to get his daughter married to a man who belongs to a good family. He accepts an alliance with someone else. What happens next forms the rest of the story.[2]
