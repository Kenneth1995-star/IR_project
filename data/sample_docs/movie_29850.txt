Vedikkai En Vadikkai

Visu and Manorama are siblings. Manorama and Kishmu have 2 sons SV Sekar, Ravi and a daughter Shruthi. They both had a difference of opinion years before as a result of which they don't talk to each other. Shruti calls Visu to get her married to her lover. Meanwhile Manorama takes the help of Visu to get her eldest son, S V Sekar married to Rekha. Ravi cheats Pallavi and ditches her. How Visu helps all these people and solves their problems is the story.
