Super Kudumbam

Arun (Prabhu Ganesan) and Hari (Vivek) are close friends and bank employees. Arun and Abhirami (Prathyusha) fall in love with each other. Shanthi (Roja) is a pickpocket who accidentally comes across Arun. Arun and Shanthi act as husband and wife to get a home for rent and also to impress the father of Hari's lover, who supports inter-caste marriage. Shanthi becomes pregnant by Arun and its revealed during the marriage of Hari. After many events he accepts Shanthi along with her sister's children whom she foster cares.
