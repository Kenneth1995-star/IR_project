Kannathal

Kannatha (Neena) is a goddess believer and she is treated as a goddess by the villagers. She lives with her father Shanmugam Pillai (Delhi Ganesh) who is a nadaswaram player and ardent believer of goddess, her mother (Fathima Babu) and her four little sisters.
Chinna Durai (Karan) is the son of rich Zamindar Mohana Sundaram (Manivannan) and Ranganayaki (Vadivukkarasi). He is a womanizer and has bad habits. One day, Chinna Durai sees Kannatha in a Hindu festival and wants to have sex with her. So he decides to get married with her. Kannatha's father Shanmugam Pillai, who is in deep debt, accepts for the marriage. Chinna Durai and Kannatha get married.
After the marriage, Chinna Durai rapes her and begins to mistreat the innocent girl. When Shanmugam Pillai comes to see Kannatha, Chinna Durai ridicules him. Feeling guilty to destroy his daughter's life, Shanmugam dies from a heart attack. Kannatha decides to accommodate her mother and sisters in their big house. Mohana Sundaram and Ranganayaki humiliate them as much as possible to expel them in addition to this Chinna Durai sexually harasses Kannatha's mother. They cannot bear the mental torture so Kannatha's mother and the four girls commit suicide.
Soon, Kannatha becomes pregnant. One night, Chinna Durai, Mohana Sundaram and Ranganayaki beats the innocent girl to death and incinerate her. They claim that this was an accident. Later, Chinna Durai gets married with his relative Seetha Lakshmi (Indhu). Seetha Lakshmi is a talkative and naive village girl. Kannatha becomes a goddess and takes revenge on the culprits.
What transpires later forms the crux of the story.
