Therkathi Kallan

Because he wishes to acquire a plot of land to be autonomous, Kallan (Vijayakanth), a nice villager tries his luck by wanting to be a movie star in the capital, and becomes finally stuntman. His path crosses that charming but very arrogant feminist Radha (Radhika) and wealthy heiress. He marries her further to a compromise. The couple quickly collides due to their excess big social difference but especially very fast realizes that it shares not at all the same values. And their troubles are only beginning, rather funny at first. But the crossing time, their married life becomes a disaster...
