Rajesh Nair

Shikha Samuel (Rima Kallingal), along with her husband Jayakrishnan (Vijay Babu), has chosen of all places in the world, Uganda, to find refuge in, after her family in Kerala objects to their marriage. The couple is leading a happy life with their daughter, Meenakshi (Meenu) .Jayakrishnan is employed as a manager in a coffee shop and Shikha runs a designer boutique of her own. She soon sets up a boutique of her own in the African country. The tranquillity of their life is shattered when Shikha is arrested on the charges of committing two murders, one of the victim is the daughter of the local Mayor, who has pledged to keep her in jail for the rest of her life. Jayakrishnan meets Parthipan through his working area owner Joju.Parthipan gives tips and materials for Rima to get out of jail. After come out of jail, Jayakrishnan and Rima finds out that Parthipan is a local Don who was behind the murders, now want to trap them. Jayakrishnan who already informed local police of Parthipan evil plans now gets help from local police. Parthipan gets arrested.
