Silambarasan, Divya Spandana, Kalabhavan Mani

The movie is about Gurumoorthy "Guru" (Silambarasan), who falls in love with Anjali (Divya Spandana), and incurs the wrath of her father (Kalabhavan Mani). Whether Guru succeeds in marrying Anjali, with the help of his college mates, forms the rest of the story.
