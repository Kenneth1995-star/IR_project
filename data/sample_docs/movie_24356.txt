Bewafa

It depicts the story of a poor girl, Roopa (Nargis) who is forced by her alcoholic uncle to work. When she can't meet her uncle's demand to earn money, she is removed from the house by her uncle when her neighbour Raj (Raj Kapoor) comes to her aid and loans her some money, but despite it she finds it difficult to make ends meet, and then she meets Ashok, with whom she later sells paintings, but then Raj reenters her life, the story follows the love triangle between Roopa, Raj and Ashok.
