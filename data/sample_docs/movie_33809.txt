Koisuru Vampire

Kiira seems like an ordinary young girl, who likes to dress up. What differentiates her from others is that she is a vampire. She has never told anyone her secret including her first love, Tetsu. After her parents died when Kiira was 12 years old, she needed to live with her relatives and lost contact with Tetsu. But 8 years later, Tetsu appears at the bakery where Kiira works...[3]
