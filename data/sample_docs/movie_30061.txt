Pathavi Pramanam

The film begins with Siva (Vijayakanth) escaping from jail, Siva is a convict who failed to kill the politician R.K (Rajan P. Dev) twice. At the transfer of power, R.K will be the new prime minister, so he deploys heavy security to protect him from Siva. Before that, Siva manages to kidnap R.K and hauled him to a television station. Siva tells his past to the audience and the reason behind the kidnapping.
