Lakshmi Kataksham

The story revolves around procuring Lakshmi Bhandagaram. Both the evil forces and kind people fight to procure. Finally, Kulavardhanudu (N. T. Rama Rao), who got Padmarekha on his palm achieves it. He saves his parents and lover Hemamalini (KR Vijaya) from the control of Prachanda (Satyanarayana).
