Dil

Seenu (Nithin) is a new admit at Maharaja College of Arts and Sciences. Nandini (Neha) goes to the same college. Seenu is from a middle-class family. She is the only daughter of Gowri Shankar (Prakash Raj), who is a land mafia don.
Gowri Shankar's assistant suspects them to be lovers after he finds them dancing at the freshers celebration at college. Seenu gets beaten up badly. Irritated by this, Seenu is challenged to win Nandini's love. After few attempts, she falls in love with him. They elope and get married when Gowri Shankar tries to separate them. The story takes few turns before it ends in a happy note.
