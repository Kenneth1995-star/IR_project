Raja Saab

Born and brought up in an orphanage, Rajoo (Shashi Kapoor) dreams of living life King-size. On being forced by his employer, Prince Pratap Singh (Rajendranath), he impersonates him in front of Princess Poonam (Nanda). Soon he falls in love with her, and after initial hesitation, Poonam too starts liking him. But will Poonam keep loving him after realizing that he is not a royal prince but a pauper.
