Hum Hain Kamaal Ke

The film revolves around two physically challenged friends; Nilamber (Kader Khan) and Pitamber (Anupam Kher). Nilambar is deaf and Pitamber is blind since birth. Both friends developed special skills and also good coordination between themselves, due to which others fail to realize that they are differently abled. Jebago (Raza Murad) is head of some gangsters. Nilambar and Pitamber attempt to solve a murder mystery with series of comic mishaps.[3]
