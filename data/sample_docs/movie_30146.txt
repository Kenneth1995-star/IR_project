Seethanam

After spending many years in jail, Muthu Manikkam (Prabhu) is back to his village and wants to take revenge on a corrupt politician Marimuthu (Mansoor Ali Khan). Soon, he clashes with advocate Radha (Ranjitha) until she knows his tragic past. It is revealed that Muthumanikkam's wife was killed by Marimuthu. In the end, Muthumanikkam kills Marimuthu.
