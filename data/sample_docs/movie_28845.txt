Lucky Di Unlucky Story

After Lucky (Gippy Grewal) is taken on a vacation for his birthday by his three friends, Dimpy (Binnu Dhillon), Brar (Jaswinder Bhalla) and Sidhu (Gurpret Ghuggi). A murder takes place and the four friends are the main suspects in their own eyes. Then a funny sequence of events takes place and finally they find out the real killer and the real victim.
