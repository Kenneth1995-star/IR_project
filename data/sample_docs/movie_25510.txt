Yateem

The story revolves around a police officer who kills a bandit and adopts his orphan son. The boy becomes a police officer and falls in love with the man's daughter, Gauri played by Farha. The step-mother who is the police officer's wife falls for krishna (Sunny). Meanwhile, she is also having an affair with Girivar (Danny). Krishna rejects the step mother. In anger she tells her husband that Krishna raped her. Shiv Kumar (Inspector) has Krishna thrown into jail. The step mother now arranges for Gauri, who is in love with Krishna to marry Girivar. Krishna breaks out from jail to prevent this. He crashes the wedding and runs off with Gauri, hoping to prove his innocence.
