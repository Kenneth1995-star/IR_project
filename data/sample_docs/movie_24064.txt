Mon Bole Priya Priya

Arjun (Raaj) travels to the city to seek admission in a college. Rich girl Priya (Pamela) is gifted a car without brakes by her uncle. Priya runs the car over Arjun and Arjun loses his memory. Priya feels sad for Arjun and romance begins to brew between the two. Ashish Vidyarthi plays the role of a good cop.[3]
