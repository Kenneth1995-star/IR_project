Delightful Forest

The legendary fighter Wu Song (Ti Lung) is sent to prison in Mengzhou province after murdering his sister-in-law and her lover. There he meets with the prison officer Shih En (Tien Ching), who saved Wu from the baton punishment required for new prisoners. Wu learns that Shih's restaurant named the "Delightful Forest" has been occupied by Chiang Chung (Chu Mu) and now he seeks his revenge.
