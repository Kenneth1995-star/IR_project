Sharara

Sharara is an action film starring Raaj Kumar and Mithun Chakraborty in the lead roles, well supported by Hema Malini in a double role, Tina Munim, Amjad Khan, Danny Denzongpa and Ranjeeta Kaur. Actress Hema Malini is understood to have produced the film. The title song of the film was very popular when released. The film received positive reviews at the time of its release mainly due to the star-studded cast and great acting performances by the stars.
