Vitthala Shappath

Krishna is a son of his parents but he forgets the main object of life. He enjoys his own way by disobeying the guidelines of his parents who are devotee of Lord Vitthala. Krishna goes on wrong path and result of which he faces a drastic problem and after realizing his mistakes, he overcomes from his bad nature. He started to full fill motive of his father and Naina help him in his work. During the period Krishna and Naina fall in love of each other. At one instance situation came before him is that he has to choose any one out of two things, one is motive of his father and second is Naina's love. What he will choose? Vijay is the hero.[2]
