Pavana Ganga

Ganga is a village girl who runs away with her brother Narsimha because of her aunt mistreating her. She comes to Banglore in search of a job and after several years she falls in love with Annaya(srinath) Narsimhas friend. Narsimsha gets married to Yamuna a young business tycoons daughter. Yamuna does not like Gangas interference in her married life and so asks her to leave the house. Will she leave the house or stay? Watch this movie to know more
