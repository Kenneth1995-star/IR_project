Detective

The film introduces Mohan Kumar (Suresh Gopi), a young political firebrand whose power and influence are on the rise. His wife Reshmi (Sindhu Menon) reportedly commits suicide. Since the couple was not known to have had any marital discord, the police are entrusted with the task of unraveling the circumstances of her death. The government orders a high-level inquiry. A team of officers headed by Shyam Prasad (Suresh Gopi in dual role) are tasked to find out the truth behind the mysterious death.
