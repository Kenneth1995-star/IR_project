Dil Rangeela 

Anuradha (Priyanka Rao), daughter of hotel owner (Achyuth Kumar), is in love with hotel chef Preetham (Ganesh). On his visit to Goa, Preetham meets Khushi (Rachita Ram) who impresses him. He helps Khushi get over her failed romance with Vicky. On the other side, Anuradha is preparing for her engagement with Preetham. On the day of the engagement, Anuradha misbehaves with Preetham's mother. This makes him to leave her and come back to Khushi. The story takes a curious turn when he sees Khushi with her estranged boyfriend. The rest is high drama which ends on a happy note.
