Rakshadhikari Baiju Oppu

Rakshadikari Baiju Oppu explores the story of a funny and good-hearted man Baiju, portrayed by Biju Menon, who is a Government employee and a patron or the Rakshadhikari of Brothers Club in Kumbalam. The club doesn't have any office but functions under a tree, where relationships are made and the warmth and joy it radiates. The members play mainly cricket. There is no definite plot for the film, just routine incidents which happen in our day-to-day life.
