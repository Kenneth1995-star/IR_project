Dhairyam

Dhairyam movie is about Ajay (Ajay Rao) a state level rank student who comes from a lower middle class back ground is always in financial crunch,[5] but his parents even though poor could somehow manage to give him good education
Meanwhile, Ajay falls in love with Parimala, whose parents are very practical about relationships imposes many term's and condition to get married to him, but Ajay is already in too much of burden as he has to arrange money for his father's cancer operation, so the story is all about how he manages these hurdles.
