Sakshyam

The movie sketches through the life of an Army Man enriched with so many emotional sequences. Brigadier Rajashekharan Nambiar is a strict and tough officer in Indian Army. Dr. Sunny is his neighbor in native now he is in Army too. Susanna (Gouthami)is a typical Army Nurse who has so much liabilities which hooks her down. Movie starts as a flash back. Rajashekaran gets a call from hospital saying that Susanna's health conditions are getting bad. He goes to convent where his daughter (Manju Warrior) studies. He takes her to mother. On the way he opens the hidden memories about Susanna, whereas he told her she was no more. The Movie goes to climax with the revealing of the flash back. Rajashekharan was a strict officer but weak hearted to woman. He always flirts with nurses and other who are in the camps. Also he was a drunken man and a massive smoker. One day he see Susan on and tries the same. But she doesn't pay attention to him. Which makes him more intense to her. Later he manages Sunny to convince her. They got married. Rajashekharan was from a royal family, so his family members not much interested with new spouse. When the time passes they gets closer to her. Meanwhile, new irritations starts to bud in between new couple. Even after one year they don't have a child. Rajshekharan gets hard to her, she tries to suicide, but survives. He understands his guilty and the story again goes good, they have a baby girl. too. When all are going good some unexpected tragedies blasts the story lines.
