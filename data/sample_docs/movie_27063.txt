Anaarkali of Aarah

Anaarkali of Aarah is the story of Anaarkali (Swara Bhaskar), who makes a living by performing on double meaning songs in public functions. During one of her stage shows, Anaarkali comes across Vice Chancellor Dharmender Chauhan (Sanjay Mishra) who tries to molest her. This leads to a conflict between the two where Dharmender holding a position of power has an upper hand but Anaarkali refuses to give in to the challenges thrown in her life and instead decides to fight back.
