Brundavanam

Ravi (Rajendra Prasad) and Latha Devi (Ramya Krishna) fall in love while helping their friends( Bhagyalakshmi (actress) Alias Bhagyasri ). Radha Kumari and Raavi Kondal Rao played the roles of mother and father of Ravi (Rajendra Prasad). Lakshmi (Anjali Devi) and Jagannadham (Gummadi) are Grandmother and Grandfathers of Ravi (Rajendra Prasad). Panakalu (Satyanarayana), father of Latha Devi(Ramya Krishna) had cheated Lakshmi (Anjali Devi) and her husband Jagannadham (Gummadi) and also illegally occupied their house named "Brundavanam". Knowing this fact Ravi (Rajendra Prasad) promises his grandmother (Anjali Devi) that he would regain the house. He enters the house as a tenant and creates lots of troubles to Panakala Swami (Satyanarayana) and finally succeeds in getting Brundavanam back to his grandmother.
