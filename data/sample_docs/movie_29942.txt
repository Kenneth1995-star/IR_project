Innisai Mazhai

Vijay (Neeraj), Mamta (Parveen), Michael (Vivek) and Saravanan (Sudhakar) are members of a music troupe. Michael and Saravanan are in love with Mamta while Mamta loves Vijay. Vijay hates love and rejects her love, shaken by his words, Mamta tries to commit suicide. Vijay's father brought him up alone and he doesn't even know his mother. So his father reveals that his mother is in fact Michael's mother. Finally, Vijay understands Mamta's love and accepts her love.
