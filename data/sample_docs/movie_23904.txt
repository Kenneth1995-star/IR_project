Bipasha

The storyline goes when Uttam Kumar the young engineer meets Suchitra Sen in today's Mithon. Their acquaintance soon reaches a stage when the young couple decide to get married. However, on the night of her marriage to Uttam Kumar the latter fails to turn up. Suchitra later finds that there were some rumours about the character of Uttam's mother played by Chaya Devi. The film revolves round the journey of Uttam Kumar in redeeming his mother's innocence and chastity.
