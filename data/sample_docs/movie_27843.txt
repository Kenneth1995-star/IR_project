Vellithira

A teenager girl named Thatha (Navya Nair) is living with her blacksmith brother Vakkathi Vasu (Kalabhavan Mani) and his children after the death of her sister in law. Style Raj (Prithviraj Sukumaran) is a guy who moves from place to place with a projector and shows movie to public on the run for a living. He meets Thatha and falls in love. Vasu discovers that Raj is actually the murderer of the landlord who killed his father. In the end Raj is sent to jail.
