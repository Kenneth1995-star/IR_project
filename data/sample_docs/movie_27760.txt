Summer Palace

Mohanraj (Krishna Kumar) and his wife Thara (sindhu) and their friends come to summer palace for celebrating their honeymoon. Summer palace was very dangerous place. One day Mohan Raj became possessed by a ghost. Its name was Gazzal (Ansil). Gazzal was killed by Thara's grandfather (Devan). The ghost was awaiting her grandfather's arrival. In the end, the ghost kills her grandfather and then leaves Mohanraj's body.
