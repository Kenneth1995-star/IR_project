Senior Mandrake

Senior Mandrake is the sequel to the film Junior Mandrake. Kuttan (Jagathy Sreekumar) has become enviably rich, and has disposed of the ill-fated statue. But soon it finds its way back to the money lender's doorstep. Without any further delay, the world starts crumbling around him, and he frantically scurries around to hand it over to some unfortunate soul.
