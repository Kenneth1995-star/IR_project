Needhikkuppin Paasam

Gopal (M.G.R.) is an honest lawyer who holds law & order above family and emotions. He meets a simple village girl named Gauri (Saroja Devi) and falls in love with her. But trouble brews in the form of Muthayya (Nambiar) who sets his wicked eyes on Gauri. In order to marry Guari, Muthayya murders her father (M.R. Radha) and Gopal's mother is framed for the murder. Struggling between family and justice, can Gopal save his mother? Will he triumph in his love?
