Chettu Kinda Pleader

A lawyer without practice is humorously called as Chettu Kinda Pleader. Balaraju (Rajendra Prasad) plays the title role in this film directed by Vamsy. Gopalkrishna (Saratbabu), a rich businessman gets killed by goons and his widow requests Balaraju to take-up the case as no other lawyer is ready to help her. Sayrabaiah (Gollapudi Maruti Rao) claims that Sujatha is not even married to Gopal Krishna. How Balaraju succeeds to unfold the mystery behind the death and what obstacles he faces are the contents of this comedy crime thriller.[4]
