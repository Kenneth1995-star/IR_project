Mini-Dora SOS

This short-film is set in the future where the main characters of Doraemon are all grown up and have kids. Minidora (mini Doraemon) accidentally gets delivered to Nobita's future house. The sons of Gian, Suneo and Nobita play with Minidora with the special goods he brings out of his pocket. But trouble occurs when they run away from Dorami who came to retrieve Minidora.
