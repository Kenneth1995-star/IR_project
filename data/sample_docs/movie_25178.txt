Sansani: The Sensation

Two persons namely Ajay and Prem come in a remote village. They go for night staying at nearby guest house from the railway station in the foggy night. Surprisingly series of murders happen immediately after their arrival and at the same time, in every night a sari clad lady roams in the area of burial ground. The police suspect Ajay and arrest him for the charge of murders but he escapes from police custody to solve the murder mystery.
