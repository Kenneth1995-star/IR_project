Sasu Cha Swayamwar

The frustrated Mohan Seeks an eligible groom for his interfering Sasu.[clarification needed] that he has inherited in his dowry. The unwanted dowry takes his life through various levels of hell so he arranges a swayamwar and story starts happening. To palm of his sasu he finds three bachelors.[clarification needed] This story is all about how the Sasu gets married and lead to an unexpected ending.
