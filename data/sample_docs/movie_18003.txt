Tobuo Bhalobashi

Completing graduation, before taking the charge of the family business, A Sangram an honest and active protester against injustice goes to Gazipur at his friend's wedding party. At that party, he is introduced to a naughty girl Sunayana. He is in love with Sunayana but he doesn't propose to her for scaring of her father. One day when Sunayana's father became ill, Sangram has to go to bring some medicine while hartal is running that was called by a terrorist Lal. Sangram looks over the hartal, involves in conflict with terrorist Lal. Lal wants to pay back.
