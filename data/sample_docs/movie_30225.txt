Pasamulla Pandiyare

Paandiyar is a genuine person adopted by Nesamani Family. The family migrates to Malaysia leaving the house under Paandiyar and other properties under Eashwara Moorthi. Vellaiyamma is a talented palm reader who falls for Paandiyar. Neesamani’s daughter Dhanalkshmi returns to India with her husband Nagaraja nad daughter Revathi.Revathi fall in love with Pandiyan. Vellaiyamma reads Dhanalkshmi’s palm and finds her untold secret story. So Nagaraja Kills Vellaiyamma associates with Eashwara Moorthi. Innocent Paandiyar gets arrested for Vellaiyamma’s Murder. Rest of the story revolves around how paandiyar proves his innocence at court.
