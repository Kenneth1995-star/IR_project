Soundtrack of Passion

The film tells the story of the pursuit of love and freedom. Vita, employee of a private detective agency, falls in love with a tall blue-eyed stranger whom she met on an island located in the center of an expansive lake in a Moscow suburb. All-consuming passion arises between them. Blinded by it, Vita does not suspect that Adam is the man whom she wiretapped for her work and that soon she will have to eliminate him.[2]
