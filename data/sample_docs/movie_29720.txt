Raman Sreeraman

Vijay is a well-known good samaritan businessman. One the day after his wedding, he is stopped by his wife ex-lover (Sathyaraj) Shankar and wishes them in sarcastic manner. Soon Shankar starts black-mailing Vidya often. Vidya (Jyothi) denies that they were ex-college-mates and kept quiet because of Shankar is brother of Vidya friend, Saradha. However, Vijay do not believe Shankar stories and believes his wife Vidya. He also employees Shankar in his company as assistant manager and inform Vidya that Shankar will not disturb them again. One day Vijay receives phone call while playing tennis with his Judge Friend (SriKanth). He receives a news that his cashier was murdered by Shankar. Shankar denies the accusation and confess that it was Vijay that actually commit the murder.
Vidya who later decide to help her friend Saradha, meet Shankar in the prison. Shankar tells Vidya that he changed into good person the moment after Vijay offers him a job. However, since he see Vijay murdered the cashier by Shankar himself and escapes with this motorcycle plate number TMC 7979, he denies Vidya ideas that Vijay is a good person. After Vidya returns home, Vijay inform, that his motorcycle number plate TMC 7979 missing. Soon Vidya start suspecting Vijay, her husband. One night, Vijay shown to be drunkard and having affair with Saradha. Saradha took this opportunity to take revenge towards Vijay. Later, Vidya receives call from the hotel, that VIjay is heavily drunken and ask to pick him. However, once Vidya reach the hotel, she is informed that Vijay went back with his wife. This further confused Vidya.
What happen next? Is Vijay is the true murder? Is he pretend to be good person or is it Shankar who commit the murder?
