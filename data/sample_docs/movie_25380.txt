Shiva Ka Insaaf

Bhola's parents get killed by Jaffna. He is then raised by 3 men. When he grows to a youth, he becomes powerful Shiva. He leaves his village and goes to a city to work as a journalist where he meets Nisha. There he lives the life of Bhola. When she finds his reality, they fall in love with each other. At the end, he kills Jaggan, who killed his parents.
