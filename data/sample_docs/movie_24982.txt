Khalifa

Rajender is a conman. He promises his girlfriend marriage after finding out about her pregnancy. He leaves his girlfriend, taking away all her savings. Kamla commits suicide. Kamla's brother Vikram decides to kill Rajendra. Vinod, who is of a good character, now encounters his look-alike Rajendra. Hence, Vinod's life is now in a complicated situation. Eventually, all confusion is solved and the movie leads to a happy ending.[1]
