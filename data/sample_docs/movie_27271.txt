Chandramuki Pranasakhi

Susheel (Ramesh Aravind) falls in love with a singer, Sahana (Prema) on seeing her sing in a music programme. He then finds her among the guests at his friend's wedding and writes her a love letter. However, the letter is read by Sahana's sister, Bhavana and replies to it not taking it seriously, but later falls in love with Susheel.
