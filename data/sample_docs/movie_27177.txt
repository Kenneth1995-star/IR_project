Vasantha Geetha

Vasanth, an insurance agent stays with his friend Srinivas Murthy's family. He saves a businessman Ashwath from thugs and also has a scuffle with his arrogant girl Geetha, without knowing she is Ashwath's daughter. After few fights, Vasanth and Geetha fall in love, get married. Unable to adjust to class-differences, Marital life of the couple fills with tension. A son is born to the couple. Geetha suspects Vasanth with infidelity, who actually is tending to sick Srinivas Murthy's wife. She storms away with her son to her father's bungalow. Vasanth now has to get his life back in order and more than that clear doubts from his son's mind.
