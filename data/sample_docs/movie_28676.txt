Goshta Choti Dongraevadhi

The film focuses on the plight of farmers in maharashtra's drought hit region. Nandu is heavily indebted farmer. Rajaram, Nandu's best friend, comes to village to earn bread and butter. Rajaram, a graduated in agriculture science, feels disappointed as he did not find good job in city. He wants to make some money by selling land but his mother prevents him from doing this and the story continues.
The film establishes how poor farmers are being wronged by society and government too.[2]
Nana Patekar lent his voice to narrate few lines at the end of this film.
